Functionality
Zoom-In: Double click on country
Zoom-Out: Shift+Double click on country
MouseOver: Mouse over a country to see where its refugees orginate from

Country Colors
Gray: No data in database on country
Reds: The darker the red, the more refugees reside in that country. If it's close to white there are relatively few refugees that reside in that country.

Arrows
Size: The size of the arrow is proportional to the percentage of refugees coming from that country.
Location: The location of the arrow points where the refugees came from to where they sought asylum.
